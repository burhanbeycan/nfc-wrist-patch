    How the real fabric patch works

    NTAG213 opens the screen; sensors provide the heartbeat data.

    1. Passive demo

    NTAG213 stores a short URL. The dashboard shows a stored snapshot such as ?id=fab01 or ?b=72.

    2. Real heartbeat

    Add PPG/ECG front-end, MCU, power, and backend/BLE/dynamic NFC memory. NTAG213 alone cannot sense pulse.

    3. Fabric integration

    Laminate or sew the inlay with strain relief. Keep the antenna away from metal, conductive gel, and severe bending.
